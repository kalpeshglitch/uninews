Dear All,
 
Hope you are all staying safe and doing well.
 
We have been overwhelmed by this unstoppable chain of events.We have entered a new year with a hope to return to offices but the increasing numbers indicate that it is some time away. In retrospect, this pandemic time has been no less than a roller-coaster ride for Team WPP Unilever, from expanding our capabilities and best practices across BSPaN, while remotely working from home and delivering outstanding work, we have come a long way. Also, it brought about irreversible changes in the ways we think and work. It’s great to see how smoothly we have been able to roll out work and adopt flexibility measures, this progress made in digitalization and remote ways of working must serve as a platform for us to speed up our transformation as a company so that we will always be prepared for the unexpected.
 
 
Since almost all news these days is a reminder of tough times let me share some good news with all ….
Our winning streak continues .
Heartiest congratulations to all, on winning “ . Huge congrats to our A brave award has been won by our Brooke Bond team who challenged a taboo subject in a country with an emotive audience and from Alan Jope who supported the team in making such a daring campaign.

We’ve all been inspired by resilience shown by members of the HUL and WPP family, our partnership has come a long way. Upwards and onwards…
