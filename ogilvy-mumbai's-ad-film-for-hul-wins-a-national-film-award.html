1) Entry to be inserted in Ogilvy section:

While Ogilvy is no stranger to winning accolades at every major advertising industry award show in the world, its work for Hindustan Unilever (HUL) titled ‘The Shower’ has brought back a coveted win at the recently declared results of the 67th National Film Awards.

These awards are considered the most prestigious honour for the Indian film industry’s work in all its forms. This is a rare instance when a so-called ‘ad film’ has marked a win here. Evocatively directed by Bauddhayan Mukherji (Buddy), the film was entered by his production house Little Lamb Films. It won the ‘Best Promotional Film’ award in the Non-Feature Films category of the show.

Reacting to the win, Ogilvy India CCOs and creators of the work, Kainaz Karmakar and Harshad Rajadhyaksha say, “This work has always been deeply special and important to us. The fact that Buddy partnered us to direct this film was the best thing that happened to this project. Dealing with the all-important issue of water conservation, it took immense sensitivity to bring alive this idea. We are thankful to our brave client team at HUL for backing work like this, to our whole team at Ogilvy who have toiled on this project, and most importantly to Buddy, whose emotional investment in this project has made it the work of compelling art that was rewarded here.”

2) Mindshare awards:

HUL & Mindshare Fulcrum win big at the Festival of Media Asia (FOMA) 2021

After creating a strong mark at the shortlists, HUL & Mindshare Fulcrum have bagged some great wins across categories.
